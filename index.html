Resolve Actions

When a character attempts an action, whose failure might have consequences:

    Roll a d20 less than or equal to the value of the most appropriate ability.


Combat

Initiative

Each party rolls a d6. Highest result acts first.

Attack

Roll a d20 less than or equal to the sum of attacker's Figher Level + their Opponent's Armor class + any bonuses or penalties.

Damage

Roll for weapon damage. (Usually 1d6.)

Subtract result from opponents HP.

At 0 HP, opponent is out of the fight.

Rogue Skills

Characters who have at least one level of Rogue can attempt to use the following skills: Open Locks, Find Traps, Remove Traps, Climb Steep Walls, Hide in Shadows, and Pickpocketing.

Roll a d20 less than or equal to the sum of the rogue's Level + Armor Class + bonuses or penalities.


Spell Casting

Each time you gain a Wizard level, you get as many spells as the class level you have reached (Example: by reaching the second level of Wizard you will get two new spells).

Roll a d20 less than equal to the sum of the wizard's Level + their Armor Class + any bonuses or penalites.

If a spell hits a target, then it will deal 1d6 points of damage. If it is intended to heal then it will heal 1d6 hit points.
